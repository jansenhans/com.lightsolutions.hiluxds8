HiluX DS8 — Homey App
by Hans Jansen

Control your LightSolutions HiluX DS8 tunable white downlight directly from Homey over your local network — no cloud required.

The HiluX DS8 is a premium recessed downlight powered by Shelly, supporting tunable white light from warm (2200K) to cool daylight (6000K).

FEATURES
- Turn the light on and off
- Adjust brightness (0–100%)
- Adjust colour temperature (warm 2200K to cool daylight 6000K)
- Local network control — fast and reliable, no cloud dependency
- Auto-discovery: the app scans your network and finds your HiluX DS8 automatically during pairing

REQUIREMENTS
- A LightSolutions HiluX DS8 downlight
- Homey Pro (local network required)
- The HiluX DS8 must be connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your Homey

PAIRING
1. Make sure your HiluX DS8 is powered on and connected to your Wi-Fi network using the LightSolutions or Shelly app.
2. In Homey, go to Devices and tap the + button.
3. Find HiluX DS8 and tap it.
4. The app will automatically scan your network and find your light.
5. Select your light from the list to add it.

TIP: To avoid your light's IP address changing after a router reboot, set a DHCP reservation for the light in your router using its MAC address. You can find the MAC address on the device label or in the LightSolutions app.

SUPPORT
If you encounter any issues, you can update the light's IP address manually under the device's Settings in Homey (Advanced Settings → IP Address).
